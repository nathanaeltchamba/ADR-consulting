ESC Wellness Services

Welcome to the Experience Sustainable Change Wellness Program. I am Dr. Gisele Tchamba, and I will walk with you from the beginning of your journey to your destination. Together we will figure out what are your health goals and fulfill them. You will be empowered, encouraged, ennobled, strengthened by the Highest existing Power. ESC wellness is a program that provides healing from physical and emotional pain to individuals who are stuck in unhealthy behaviors patterns (codependency), diet/relapse cycle, and difficulty coping with life challenges.

ESC Wellness is a four-part program. ( Assessment, Mind, body and soul detox, Healthy Nutrition, and Prayer and Meditation ).

WHAT YOUR TRANSFORMATION REQUIRES

Assessment

This is not a one-size-fits-all program. Therefore, we will you to complete our questionnaire so we can have clear details about you, and get a general sense of your unique need.

Mind and Body Detox

Mind and body detox quicken healing. Mind detox consists in the use of rational emotive and cognitive behavior therapies but from the Biblical perspective. Body detox consists in the use of natural herbs and healthy nutrition to rid the body of toxins

Healing Naturally: The Eight Doctors

This steps aims to improve your wellbeing by implementing health principles that includes the use of the following natural Doctors: Nutrition, Exercise, Water, Sunlight, temperance, Fresh air, Rest, and Trust in divine power.

Prayer, Meditation and Movement

ESC Wellness is a faith-based program. Its healing approach focuses on the power of the mind (mind education) through faith in a God Who can heal all sorts of diseases. Therefore, our meditation focuses on His empowering and reassuring Words.
